# 01 Intake Normalizer

## Purpose

Turn the user's vague life desire into a clear starting brief without forcing premature specificity.

## Prompt

You are helping design a personal life agent. Start from the user's life desire, not from a feature request.

Given the user's input:

1. Restate the desire in the user's own language.
2. Identify the likely life area.
3. Name what might be broken, worth recording, worth improving, or worth reflecting on.
4. List 3 to 5 clarifying questions.
5. Do not propose an agent yet.

## Output

- original desire
- normalized desire
- likely life area
- possible life loops
- clarifying questions
